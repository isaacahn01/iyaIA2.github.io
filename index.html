Trending Recipes

Dalgona

A sugary toffee that has a light spongy texture. Popularized by the Netflix series, Squid Game, this candy is a traditional Korean snack with a hidden game in it! Tasty and fun for people of all ages!

Salmon Rice

A savory salmon dish topped with rice. Perfect for those who have leftover salmon in their fridge. Easy and simple to make with many different combinations to eat it with!

Jjapageuri

A combination of two kinds of instant ramen (Chapaghetti and Neoguri) that make for the perfect meal on a rainy day. Although it has been around a while in Korea it was popularized around the world through the Oscar-winning film Parasite.
